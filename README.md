# TerraStreak 🌿 — Personal Carbon Footprint Tracker & Sunflower Companion
TerraStreak is a gamified carbon ledger application. It integrates daily carbon footprint tracking, digital habits monitoring, smart grid scheduling, and an interactive, expressive Sunflower companion that grows, reacts, and changes styles based on your daily real-world choices.

## 🏛️ Core Pillars of TerraStreak
*   **Visual Gamification:** Connects real-world carbon tracking directly to the life-stages and emotional moods of a responsive Sunflower companion.
*   **Behavioral Habit Loops:** Incentivizes green routines using structured daily quests, weekly milestones, and monthly targets.
*   **Resource Optimization:** Guides household appliance scheduling and digital bandwidth usage based on live electricity grid clean-energy windows.
*   **Interactive Learning:** Tests and rewards ecological knowledge through a multi-tier, 30-question interactive quiz system.
---

## 🚀 Key Features
*   **Carbon Ledger & Wallet:** Log lifestyle carbon-consuming activities and replenishing offsets against a default 5.0 kg daily budget.
*   **Expressive Sunflower Companion:** Real-time breathing, morphing eyebrows, closed sleeping eyelids, and budget-exceeded wilting states.
*   **Boutique Accessory Shop:** Purchase 20 unique SVG accessories using Green Coins earned from quests and quizzes.
*   **Smart Grid Scheduler:** Schedule power-hungry household appliances during clean energy windows on the 24h GreenGrid timeline.
*   **Digital Footprint Tracker:** Monitor server emissions from online activities, such as streaming, social media, and AI queries.
*   **Streak Protection ("I Slipped Today"):** Protects your daily streak from resetting by admitting slips honestly.
---
## 📖 Other Features
### 1. Gamified Sustainability
Instead of plain graphs, carbon tracking is translated into the health of your Sunflower companion. Eco-friendly choices nurture its growth, while exceeding your carbon budget causes the companion to wilt.
### 2. Reactive Companion States
Your Sunflower dynamically changes based on your habits:
*   **Streak Growth:** The plant matures from a sprout (0–2 days) to a budding stem (3–5 days), and finally blooms (6+ days) to unlock accessories.
*   **Budget Moods:** Staying under your 5.0 kg budget keeps the Sunflower smiling with a green glow. Exceeding it causes it to wilt and turn sad until offsets are logged.
*   **Circadian Rhythm:** The plant falls asleep late at night, encouraging healthy screen-time limits.
### 3. Boutique Shop & Quizzes
*   **Boutique Accessories:** Purchase and equip 20 custom accessories (like Top Hats, DJ Headphones, and Cowboy Hats) that sway in sync with the plant's animations.
*   **Multi-Level Quizzes:** Play one daily quiz (Easy, Medium, or Hard) of 10 questions. Perfect scores yield up to 85 Green Coins to spend in the boutique.
### 4. Interactive Carbon Trackers
*   **Carbon Ledger:** A live journal of daily footprint debits and credits.
*   **Digital Tracker:** Measures emissions from streaming, gaming, and AI queries, recommending optimization tricks like video resolution downgrades.
*   **GreenGrid Advisor:** Shows clean grid windows, rewarding users for scheduling heavy appliance runs during green hours.
